## Why build a custom shortener?

I wanted branded links, custom metadata, and the ability to reject abusive payloads before they propagate.

<Callout variant="danger" title="Anti-abuse">
HCaptcha + FingerprintJS prevents bot submissions, while rate limiting lives on Vercel Edge middleware.
</Callout>
